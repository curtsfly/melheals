About Me

Hi, I’m Melanie – a Nutritional Therapy Practitioner with a passion for helping people reconnect with their bodies, their food, and their innate ability to heal.

My journey into holistic nutrition started when I struggled with fatigue and digestive issues for years, and no one could give me clear answers. It wasn’t until I discovered the power of real food and root-cause healing that I began to feel like myself again.

I believe wellness is not one-size-fits-all. As an NTP, I work with you to understand your unique story, uncover imbalances, and create a realistic plan that supports your body’s natural rhythms. Whether you’re navigating chronic symptoms, looking to boost energy, or simply wanting to feel better in your skin, I’m here to support you every step of the way.

  • Real food that nourishes deeply
  • Bio-individuality—because no two bodies are the same
  • A holistic lens that looks at the whole person, not just symptoms
  • Sustainable changes that feel empowering, not overwhelming

When I’m not working with clients, you can usually find me digging in my garden, reading a good book, or exploring the local farmers market.

I’d be honored to walk with you on your wellness journey.
